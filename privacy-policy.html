Data We May Collect

Depending on the specific application and its functionality, we may collect only minimal data necessary for operation, including:

Collected information may be used to:

Some applications may use trusted third-party services (such as analytics, subscription management, or payment processing). These services process data in accordance with their own privacy policies and applicable data protection standards.

Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user data. Access to data is limited to persons who need it strictly for application operation and support.
However, no method of data transmission or storage is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

Emails and Notifications

Some applications may send service-related messages, updates, or feature announcements.
If such communication is optional, users can opt out at any time or contact us at support@someapps.dev.

Data Retention

We retain data only for as long as necessary to provide the application's functionality or comply with legal obligations.
Users may request deletion of their data by contacting support@someapps.dev, unless retention is required by law.

Policy Updates
